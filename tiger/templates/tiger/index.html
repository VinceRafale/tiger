Takeout Tiger

Big-chain features for local restaurant budgets

Takeout Tiger delivers an all-in-one solution for small restaurants to manage their online presence.

Create beautiful online menus

Publish your menu online with just a few clicks. Need to change something? Your edits take effect immediately. You can customize the look and feel of your menu and even add photos. Our Google-friendly menu structure will help drive new visitors to your site.

Take orders online

Cut down on five-minute phone orders. With your new Takeout Tiger menu, your customers can easily order online for takeout, dine-in, or delivery. You can optionally accept payment online using Authorize.net.

Network with your customers

Whether you are new to social media or a seasoned veteran, we will help you engage with your client base. Use Twitter and Facebook to automatically broadcast menu changes and updates.

Market more effectively

Keep your regulars in the loop and spend less time doing it. Send your specials, your complete menu, or an online coupon to your customers by fax, e-mail, or both.
